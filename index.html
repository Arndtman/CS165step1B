Rig By Gerrymandering

Alexander Arndt

acarndt@ucsc.edu

Github link to web files

Objective: Gerrymandering is a tactic used by political parties to essentially "guarantee" wins in swings states years before an actual election occurs. This is done, specifically, by manipulating the way the votes are tallied. The goal here is to outline how the Republican party shaped the voting boundaries in specific swing states in the 2016 election to help influence a Trump presidency. Once again, please read the DISCLAIMER at the bottom.
